✦ The Problem

Every day,
silent losses on dairy farms.

Without continuous monitoring, the earliest signs of disease go unnoticed — until milk quality and profitability both suffer.

Issue 01
🦠
Late Mastitis Detection

Often spotted too late — milk quality has already dropped, contaminated batches are lost, and treatment costs spike. The damage is done before the farmer even knows.

Issue 02
👁️
Manual, Impossible Monitoring

Watching every cow 24/7 is humanly impossible. Faint early signals — a slight temperature rise, a change in rumination — get missed entirely until they become visible crises.

Issue 03
🥛
Rejected Milk & Lost Revenue

Contaminated batches mean lost revenue and lost trust from buyers. A single undetected infection can result in a rejected tank — a devastating financial blow.

Issue 04
📵
No Data, No Decisions

Without digital data, all decisions rely on intuition alone. There is no history, no pattern recognition, no baseline — just guesswork. The modern farm deserves better.

✦ The Solution

One integrated system,
from sensor to decision.

MooScan collects, transmits, analyzes and alerts — on a closed loop, for every cow, every day.

Step 01

IoT Sensors

Real-time capture of milk, health and behavioral signals directly from each cow's smart collar.

Step 02

Secure Cloud

Encrypted ingestion, storage and processing at scale. Your data is safe, accessible and always available.

Step 03

AI Engine

Anomaly detection, classification and risk forecasting. Every cow gets her own baseline — deviations are flagged before they become crises.

Step 04

Alerts & Action

Clear, prioritized recommendations delivered to the farmer's dashboard and mobile app. Act fast, act right.

✦ AI Engine

AI turns signals
into insight.

Every cow gets her own baseline. MooScan flags what deviates — before it's too late.

Anomaly Detection
Spot abnormal cross-signal patterns across milk, health and behavioral streams simultaneously.
Classification
Each cow is continuously classified: Healthy · Suspicious · Intervene.
Prediction
Forecast mastitis risk windows and other disease events 48–72 hours before clinical symptoms appear.
Early Warning
Alerts delivered before clinical symptoms appear — giving farmers and vets time to act effectively.
Cow Status Indicators
🐄
● Healthy
All signals within personal baseline. No action needed. Continue routine monitoring.
🐄
● Suspicious
One or more signals deviating from baseline. Increased monitoring frequency triggered automatically.
🐄
● Intervene
Cross-signal anomaly confirmed. Immediate alert sent to farmer and vet with recommended action.
"Every cow gets her own baseline. MooScan flags what deviates from it — not from a generic average — making detection far more precise and individual."
