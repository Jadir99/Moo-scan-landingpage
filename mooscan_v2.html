The Process

From sensor to action in seconds.

MooScan's four-step pipeline turns raw sensor data into clear, actionable decisions for every farmer.

01

Sense

Lightweight IoT sensors on each cow collect temperature, activity, rumination, and milking data every 15 minutes.

→
02

Transmit

Data is sent wirelessly via LoRaWAN or 4G to our farm gateway, then encrypted and uploaded to the MooScan cloud.

→
03

Analyze

Our AI engine compares incoming data against each cow's individual baseline and herd-wide patterns to detect anomalies.

→
04

Alert

AI-ranked alerts reach the farmer instantly via mobile app, SMS, or dashboard — with a recommended action for each case.
