PROJECT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

MooScan is a project by YaneCode Digital, presented at SIAM 2026.
A smart IoT & AI monitoring system designed specifically for dairy cow management.

================================================================================
THE PROBLEM
================================================================================

Every day, silent losses on dairy farms.

Without continuous monitoring, the earliest signs of disease go unnoticed — 
until milk quality and profitability both suffer.

Key Issues:
1. LATE MASTITIS
   - Often spotted too late — milk quality has already dropped.

2. MANUAL MONITORING
   - Watching every cow 24/7 is impossible; faint signals get missed.

3. REJECTED MILK
   - Contaminated batches mean lost revenue and lost trust.

4. NO DATA
   - Without digital data, decisions rely on intuition alone.

================================================================================
THE SOLUTION
================================================================================

One integrated system, from sensor to decision.

MooScan collects, transmits, analyzes and alerts — on a closed loop, 
for every cow, every day.

THE PIPELINE (4-Step Process):

01. IoT SENSORS
    - Real-time capture of milk, health and behavioral signals

02. SECURE CLOUD
    - Encrypted ingestion, storage and processing at scale

03. AI ENGINE
    - Anomaly detection, classification and risk forecasting

04. ALERTS & ACTION
    - Clear recommendations delivered to the farmer's dashboard

================================================================================
THE DATA
================================================================================

Three data worlds — one view of the cow.

Fusing milk, health and behavioral streams reveals what no single signal can.

MILK DATA:
- Yield (L/day)
- Conductivity
- Temperature
- Flow rate
- Somatic cell proxy

HEALTH DATA:
- Body temperature
- Heart & respiration
- Rumen activity
- Udder condition
- Disease markers

BEHAVIORAL DATA:
- Rumination time
- Activity / steps
- Standing vs lying
- Feeding & drinking
- Heat signs

================================================================================
ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

From signal on the ground, to farmer decision.

01. EDGE
    Sensors on/around the cow

02. GATEWAY
    Wi-Fi / LoRa / 4G

03. CLOUD
    Secure storage & processing

04. AI
    Analytics & predictions

05. APP
    Dashboard & alerts

================================================================================
ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
================================================================================

AI turns signals into insight.

Every cow gets her own baseline. MooScan flags what deviates — before it's too late.

AI CAPABILITIES:

1. ANOMALY DETECTION
   - Spot abnormal cross-signal patterns

2. CLASSIFICATION
   - Healthy · suspicious · intervene

3. PREDICTION
   - Forecast mastitis risk windows

4. EARLY WARNING
   - Before clinical symptoms appear

COW STATUS INDICATORS:
- HEALTHY (Green indicator)
- SUSPICIOUS (Yellow indicator)
- INTERVENE (Red indicator)
