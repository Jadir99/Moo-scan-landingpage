About MooScan

Smart Monitoring from Edge to Insight

Bridging the gap between traditional dairy farming and modern technology

Transforming Dairy Care with AI

MooScan collects, transmits, analyzes and alerts — on a closed loop, for every cow, every day. Our integrated system brings real-time monitoring to every aspect of dairy farm management.

At MooScan, we believe that great dairy production begins with honest monitoring. Guided by IoT sensors and AI algorithms, our system is built on values of sustainability, animal welfare, and transparency.

Monitored Data

Three Dimensions of Health

Comprehensive data fusion for complete cow wellness assessment

🥛 Milk Data

Yield: Liters per day
Conductivity: Detect inflammation
Temperature: Real-time monitoring
Flow Rate: Milking efficiency
Somatic Cells: Quality indicator

❤️ Health Data

Body Temperature: Fever detection
Heart Rate: Cardiovascular monitoring
Rumen Activity: Digestive health
Udder Condition: Mastitis early detection
Disease Markers: Preventive alerts

🚶 Behavioral Data

Rumination Time: Feeding patterns
Activity Level: Movement tracking
Standing/Lying: Comfort assessment
Feeding & Drinking: Appetite changes
Heat Signs: Reproductive timing
